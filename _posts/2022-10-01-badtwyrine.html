An illustration based off of the following dialogue from the videogame series Pathologic:

"Farkhad was a guild fellow of mine… His ardour was disproportionate to his talent.

Yes, he deserved to die. But I didn’t kill him. They say he fell down from a great height and broke his neck. I’ve also heard a rumor that he got lethally intoxicated with bad twyrine…"

Used as the aesthetic basis for the project "Death of Farhad".
